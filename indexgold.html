Product Details
What makes Coconut Collagen Boost special?
Coconut Collagen Boost is a 100% plant-based collagen booster! It supports your body's natural collagen production using superfood ingredients like bamboo extract, hyaluronic acid, and biotin.
What does Coconut Collagen Boost taste like?
Coconut Collagen Boost tastes like a creamy, vanilla-coconut cloud.
Does Coconut Collagen Boost contain collagen?
Coconut Collagen Boost does not contain any actual collagen. It's powered by a blend of superfoods that naturally promote your body's own collagen production!
How does vegan collagen work?

Our bodies are full of collagen, and totally capable of producing it on their own! Coconut Collagen Boost supports this natural production with a blend of superfoods and other key ingredients. In each 2 tsp serving, the following ingredients support your collagen production:

Bamboo Extract: 200 mg per serving of 2 tsp. Coconut Collagen Boost is formulated with bamboo extract, which is the richest natural source of silica, a building block for collagen production.

Hyaluronic Acid: 80 mg per serving of 2 tsp. Hyaluronic acid supports skin hydration from within.

Biotin: 1000 mcg per serving of 2 tsp. Biotin (aka vitamin B7) strengthens the keratin in your hair and nails.

Is Coconut Collagen Boost cruelty-free?
Yes! Coconut Collagen contains no actual collagen, which can only be found in animal sources. Most hyaluronic acid is sourced from animal products, but we source a hyaluronic acid for Coconut Collagen Boost that's derived from fermented vegetables to keep our formula 100% plant-based and cruelty-free.
